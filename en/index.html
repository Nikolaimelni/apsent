AP$ENT

Arseniy Kislyak, better known as Ap$ent, is a Belarusian musician. He was born in Kharkiv but moved to Belarus in his childhood, where he began his musical career. Before fully dedicating himself to music, he tried his hand at business and finance.
In 2018, he created the project "AP$ENT," which immediately captured the public's attention with its rhythms and deep lyrics. He is not afraid to address sharp social issues in his tracks, making his music particularly relevant.
